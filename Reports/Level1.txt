Task 1: Top Cuisines Analysis

Objective:
To identify the most common cuisines available in the dataset.

Steps Performed:
- Loaded dataset using pandas
- Split multiple cuisines using string split
- Counted frequency using value_counts()

Output:
Top cuisines such as Japanese, Asian, and European were found.

Conclusion:
The dataset shows that certain cuisines are more popular and frequently offered.


Task 2: City Analysis

Objective:
To find which city has the most restaurants and highest ratings.

Steps Performed:

- Used groupby() to calculate average ratings

Output:
One city had the highest number of restaurants and another had the highest rating.

Conclusion:
Restaurant distribution and ratings vary across cities.
